### Job Title: Volunteer Social Media Coordinator (Non-Profit)

### Job Overview:

We are looking for a passionate and committed Volunteer Social Media Coordinator to join our team at Muslim Hikers Ontario. As a valued member of our team, the Volunteer Social Media Coordinator will play a crucial role in shaping and implementing our social media strategy. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals who are enthusiastic about making a positive impact in their community and supporting our mission of promoting outdoor activities in the Muslim Community.

Responsibilities:

1. Social Media Strategy and Planning:

- Contribute to the development and execution of a comprehensive social media strategy aligned with organizational goals.
2. Content Creation and Curation:

- Assist in creating engaging and relevant content for various social media platforms, sharing impactful stories and updates.
3. Community Engagement:

- Monitor social media channels, engaging with our online community through comments, messages, and mentions.
4. Campaign Coordination:

- Support the coordination and execution of social media campaigns to promote fundraising, awareness, and advocacy efforts.
5. Analytics and Reporting:

- Collaborate on the analysis of social media metrics to assess campaign effectiveness and provide insights for improvement.
Partnerships and Outreach:

6. Assist in identifying and engaging with influencers, partners, and supporters to expand the organization's reach.

### Qualifications:

Strong written and verbal communication skills.
Basic understanding of social media platforms and willingness to learn.
Creativity and a collaborative mindset.
Commitment to volunteering time to support the organization's goals.
